# DataFud

## Design Context

### Users
- **Diners** scanning a QR at their table — browsing a menu on their phone, hungry, want it fast and appetizing. Light context, daytime/evening.
- **Restaurant/soda owners** managing menu, orders and reports — used during service, on phone or laptop, needs clarity and speed.
- **SaaS owner (super admin)** — approves tenants, tracks payments. Wants an at-a-glance, trustworthy admin.

### Brand Personality
Warm · crafted · confidently premium. DataFud should feel like a well-designed gastronomic brand, not a generic SaaS template. Tagline: "QR Menus. Orders. Analytics." Logo motif: a "D" built from QR pixels + a fork.

### Aesthetic Direction
**Editorial Culinary / premium hospitality.** Magazine-grade food editorial feel: distinctive editorial serif for headings, warm cream-tinted neutrals, generous air, appetizing imagery, the QR-pixel→fork motif as a signature decorative texture. Light theme throughout (food browsed in daylight; admin used during service). Differentiator: the animated QR-pixel motif and editorial type set it apart from indigo/glassmorphism SaaS slop.

- **Colors:** forest green `#22503a` (primary) + gold `#b8923f` (accent), warm cream/stone neutrals tinted toward green. No pure black/white. OKLCH-derived steps.
- **Type:** Young Serif (display headings) + Hanken Grotesk (body/UI). Avoid Inter/Playfair/Fraunces monoculture.
- **Motion:** purposeful only. Custom easing `cubic-bezier(0.23,1,0.32,1)`. Staggered hero reveal on load. `:active` press feedback on buttons. transform/opacity only. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`.

### Design Principles
1. **No emoji as icons — ever.** SVG icons only (see `src/components/ui/icon.tsx`).
2. **Editorial hierarchy:** few sizes, high contrast, serif display + clean sans. Left-aligned, asymmetric, not everything centered or carded.
3. **Appetizing & trustworthy:** real food imagery, warm neutrals, gold used sparingly as the 10% accent.
4. **Motion with purpose:** one well-orchestrated load; micro-feedback on interaction; never decorative jank.
5. **Avoid AI tells:** no colored left-border stripes, no gradient text, no glassmorphism-everywhere, no identical icon-card grids.
